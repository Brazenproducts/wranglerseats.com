Gladiator JT Fitment Overview

Front seats share JL Wrangler architecture -- JL-compatible covers work for the front row. The rear seat is entirely unique to the Gladiator JT and sits above the rear wheel wells in a compact configuration that no other Wrangler generation shares -- do not order JLU rear covers for a Gladiator.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best seat covers for a Jeep Gladiator JT?

Bartact is the top overall pick, manufacturing complete sets including the Gladiator's unique rear seat configuration. Rough Country's neoprene Gladiator covers are the best-reviewed Amazon alternative.

Will Wrangler JL seat covers fit a Gladiator?

Front seats only. The Gladiator shares JL front-seat architecture, so JL-compatible front covers work. The rear seat is unique to the Gladiator and requires Gladiator-specific rear covers -- do not use JLU rear covers.

Do these covers fit the Gladiator Mojave or 392 special editions?

Not automatically. Special editions like Mojave and 392 may have different seat configurations -- confirm fitment with Bartact or the Amazon listing before ordering.
