What Makes This Year Range Different

The 1997–2002 TJ is two-door only — the LJ Unlimited didn't arrive until 2004. No trim level in this range came with side-curtain or seat-mounted side airbags, so airbag seam compatibility is a non-issue. Seat dimensions are essentially identical across the full 1997–2006 TJ run, so TJ-specific covers typically fit both ranges — but verify with the manufacturer before ordering.

Many 1997–2002 TJs are running high-mileage with worn-out OEM foam. A quality seat cover at this point is often paired with a seat foam replacement — worth factoring into your budget.

Fitment Notes

  • No 1997–2002 TJ trim has side airbags — airbag compatibility is not a concern for this range.
  • Two-door only — no LJ Unlimited in these years.
  • Seat dimensions are essentially the same through 2006; TJ-specific covers generally fit the full 1997–2006 run. Verify before ordering.
  • Running aftermarket high-back bucket seats? None of the covers above will fit — check with the seat manufacturer.
  • High-mileage TJs often have compressed foam under the OEM covers. Consider pairing a new cover with seat foam replacement for best results.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best seat covers for a 1997–2002 Jeep Wrangler TJ?

Bartact is the top overall pick for USA-made quality and MOLLE integration. For Amazon options, Smittybilt's neoprene TJ cover is the most reviewed and highest-rated alternative, specifically sized for 1997–2002.

Do 1997–2002 TJ Wranglers have side airbags?

No. The 1997–2002 TJ does not have side-curtain or seat-mounted side airbags on any trim level, so airbag compatibility is not a concern for this generation.

Will TJ LJ (2003–2006) seat covers fit the 1997–2002 TJ?

Generally yes — the seat profile is essentially identical across the full TJ run. TJ-specific covers typically fit 1997–2006. Verify with the manufacturer if unsure.

My TJ has aftermarket seats — will these covers fit?

No. Aftermarket high-back buckets have their own dimensions. Check directly with the seat manufacturer for compatible covers.
