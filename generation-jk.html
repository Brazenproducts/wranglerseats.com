Fitment Notes

  • JK two-door and JKU four-door have completely different rear seat configurations -- always specify your body style.
  • JK introduced side-curtain airbags -- always verify airbag compatibility on any cover before purchase.
  • JKU rear seats fold down for cargo -- quality covers must not prevent this function.
  • Bartact JK covers use Cordura 400D nylon with real mil-spec MOLLE webbing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best seat covers for a JK or JKU Wrangler?

Bartact is the top overall pick for JK and JKU. For Amazon alternatives, Smittybilt's neoprene JK covers (2013-2018) are the most reviewed option, while GIANT PANDA covers earlier 2008-2010 JK years.

Do JK Wranglers have side airbags I need to worry about?

Yes. JK (2007+) introduced side-curtain airbags. Always verify airbag compatibility on any seat cover before purchasing.

Can I use the same covers on a JK two-door and a JKU four-door?

No. JK two-door and JKU four-door have different rear seat configurations. Always specify your exact body style when ordering.
