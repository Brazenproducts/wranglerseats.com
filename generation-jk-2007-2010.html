What Makes This Year Range Different

The 2007–2010 JK introduced the four-door Unlimited (JKU) alongside the standard two-door. The interior trim received updates in 2011, so some seat covers marketed as "JK" may be sized specifically for the 2011+ refresh — always check the year range in the product listing.

Side-curtain airbags were not standard on base Wrangler and Wrangler X trims in this period. They appeared on Sahara and Rubicon trims. Check your specific trim before assuming your 2007–2010 JK does or doesn't have side airbags.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best seat covers for a 2007–2010 JK?

Bartact is the top overall pick for USA-made quality, MOLLE integration, and SAB-compatible seams. For Amazon, GIANT PANDA makes a JK-specific neoprene cover with a solid rating and early-JK specific sizing.

Do 2007–2010 JK Wranglers have side airbags?

It depends on trim. Side-curtain airbags were standard on Sahara and Rubicon but not base trims. Check your door jamb sticker or window sticker to confirm.

Will 2011+ JK seat covers fit my 2007–2010 JK?

Sometimes, but not always. Jeep updated the interior trim in 2011. Some covers are sized specifically for the updated seats. Check the product's listed year range before ordering.
