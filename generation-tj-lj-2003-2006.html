What Makes This Year Range Different

The 2003 TJ is mechanically identical to the 1997–2002. In 2004, Jeep introduced the LJ Unlimited — a stretched wheelbase version with a longer body and more rear cargo space, but the same front and rear seat dimensions as the standard TJ. TJ-specific covers fit both without modification.

Neither the TJ nor LJ came with side airbags on any trim in these years, so airbag compatibility is not a concern. If you're replacing covers on a high-mileage LJ, seat foam wear is common — worth inspecting before covering.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are LJ Unlimited seat covers different from TJ covers?

No. The LJ uses the same front and rear seat dimensions as the TJ. TJ-specific covers fit the LJ Unlimited without modification.

Do 2003–2006 TJ/LJ Wranglers have side airbags?

No. Neither the TJ nor LJ Unlimited came with side-curtain or seat-mounted side airbags. Airbag compatibility is not a concern for this generation.

Will 1997–2002 TJ covers fit my 2003–2006 TJ or LJ?

Generally yes. The seat profile is identical across the full TJ/LJ run. Verify with the manufacturer to be sure.
