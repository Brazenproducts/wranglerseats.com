What Makes This Year Range Different

Jeep refreshed the JK interior in 2011 — updated dashboard trim, revised instrument cluster, and slightly different seat bolstering compared to 2007–2010. Some seat covers labeled "JK" are specifically patterned for the pre-2011 interior. Check that the cover listing includes 2011–2012 before ordering.

Side-curtain airbags were standard on Sahara and Rubicon trims in these years, and not available on Sport and Sport S. Check your door jamb sticker to confirm your trim's airbag configuration. Bartact's SAB-compatible seam construction handles both cases safely.

Frequently Asked Questions

What changed in the 2011 JK interior?

Jeep refreshed the JK interior in 2011 with updated trim panels, a new instrument cluster design, and revised seat bolstering. Some covers sized for 2007–2010 may not fit correctly on the updated seats.

Do 2011–2012 JK Wranglers have side airbags?

Depends on trim. Standard on Sahara and Rubicon, not available on Sport/Sport S. Check your door jamb sticker to confirm.

Will 2013+ JK covers fit a 2011–2012 JK?

Generally yes — the interior was consistent from 2011 through 2018. Most covers listing "2011–2018" or "2011–2013+" will fit your 2011 or 2012.
